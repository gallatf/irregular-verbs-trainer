How verbs are selected

Each card is picked at random, but difficult verbs appear more often than easy ones. Recent answers count more than older ones — so a verb you got right three times in a row gradually fades into the background, while one you keep missing comes up more often. The same verb never appears twice in a row.

Near-miss retry (Type it mode)

If your answer is only one character wrong, or has two adjacent characters swapped (e.g. borken instead of broken), you get a second attempt. The amber border shows which field needs fixing. Getting it right on the retry counts as a correct answer.

Progress & persistence

Your progress is saved automatically in your browser after every card and restored when you reopen the page. Open the Report to see your stats and to export or import a JSON backup of your progress.
