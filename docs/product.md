# Product definitions

## Flashcard
A flashcard is one learning item representing one irregular verb.

## Card front
The initial state of the flashcard before the answer is revealed.
It shows the infinitive and may later include input fields.

## Card back
The revealed state of the flashcard showing:
- infinitive
- past simple
- past participle
- optional translation

## Known
The learner believes they recalled the answer correctly enough.

## Unknown
The learner did not recall the answer correctly.

## Session
A practice run consisting of multiple flashcards shown in sequence.

## Deck
The full collection of verbs currently eligible for practice.

## Reveal
The action that shows the correct answer on the current card.

## Typing mode
A mode where the learner types the answer before reveal.

## Spaced repetition
A future scheduling mechanism where difficult or unknown verbs reappear sooner than easy or known verbs.
