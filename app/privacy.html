3. Legal basis

We process your personal data on the following bases:

  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) — your email and password are necessary to provide the account and cross-device sync service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — storing learning progress locally in your browser so the app functions without requiring an account.

4. Data processor — Supabase

Account data and server-side progress are stored with Supabase Inc. (San Francisco, CA, USA), acting as our data processor under a Data Processing Agreement. Data transfers from the EU to Supabase are covered by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) in accordance with Art. 46 GDPR.

Supabase's privacy information: supabase.com/privacy

5. Data retention

We retain your account and progress data for as long as your account is active. Accounts and associated progress data that have had no activity for 2 years are deleted automatically.

When you delete your account using the in-app "Delete account" option, your data is removed from the live database immediately. Supabase may retain encrypted database backups for up to 7 days after deletion as part of their standard infrastructure — this is outside our direct control. Backups cannot be used to restore individual accounts.

Anonymous progress stored only in your browser (localStorage) stays on your device until you clear it manually via the "Reset" button or your browser settings.

6. Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access (Art. 15) — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification (Art. 16) — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure (Art. 17) — delete your account and all associated data using the in-app "Delete account" option, or by contacting us.
  • Portability (Art. 20) — download your progress data at any time using the "Export progress" button in the Report screen. The export includes your account details and full learning history.
  • Restriction (Art. 18) — ask us to restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Objection (Art. 21) — object to processing based on legitimate interest.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at gallatf@email.cz. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Right to complain

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection supervisory authority. In the Czech Republic, this is the Úřad pro ochranu osobních údajů (ÚOOÚ):
www.uoou.cz — Pplk. Sochora 27, 170 00 Praha 7

If you are in another EU member state, you may contact your local supervisory authority instead.
